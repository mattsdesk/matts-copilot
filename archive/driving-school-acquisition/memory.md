## Key Risks and Considerations

1. **Supervising instructor dependency:** Licensing tied to Jamal; multiple instructors qualified but owner prefers control. Need transition plan.

2. **Stock sale structure:** Requires indemnification for pre-closing liabilities. Asset sale alternative available.

3. **Staffing stability:** 4 part-time driving instructors + 1 office manager. No issues flagged, but need tenure/compensation review.

4. **No employee awareness:** Staff don't know about sale; transition communication needed post-closing.

5. **Customer concentration:** Need to assess dependency on key accounts.
