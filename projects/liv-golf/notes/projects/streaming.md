> OTT streaming infrastructure encompasses complete video delivery for live events including encoding, CDN, DRM, multi-region distribution, and connected device support. LIV Golf currently handles 30+ simultaneous streams with AI-powered auto-switching from 40+ camera feeds and scoring integration. As of February 2026, streaming is deprioritised in favour of mobile/web app development but designated as a "very fast follow" for 2026.

### Goal
Establish a scalable OTT infrastructure strategy leveraging the GlobalLogic partnership that addresses current ViewLift pain points and supports 30+ simultaneous streams with advanced camera switching and global distribution.

### Status
Streaming is at risk due to compounding issues surfaced at the South Africa event. Chris D's departure left the technical video delivery role uncovered; Andrew and Arthur are compensating but the current focus is operational, not strategic. Two RCAs are due for South Africa: a Round 1 configuration error and a Round 4 extended outage (38–56 minutes). ClipView is significantly underperforming WSC and may be dropped. Moby and ViewLift contracts both expire next year, requiring a platform decision soon. Rights are being committed without team consultation, creating a strategic gap between board requirements and fan engagement goals. A dedicated product person with audience insight capability is urgently needed.

### Key Decisions
- **March 17, 2026:** ClipView flagged as underperforming vs. WSC — drop decision pending if no improvement without extra cost
- **March 17, 2026:** Team streams identified as low financial value; concept of customisable 2–4 golfer feeds proposed as replacement
- **March 17, 2026:** Course map visualisation concept proposed (player avatars in real-time, F1-style entry point)
- **January 20, 2026:** OTT/streaming identified as future phase in GlobalLogic partnership; not immediate priority
- **February 3, 2026:** Streaming designated as "very fast follow" for 2026; exclusive GlobalLogic window through March 1
- **February 3, 2026:** Crawl-walk-run approach approved: Phase 1 (app/website), Phase 2 (streaming 2026), Phase 3 (smart venues 2027+)
