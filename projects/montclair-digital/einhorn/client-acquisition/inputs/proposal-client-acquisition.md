# Proposal: Einhorn Client Acquisition Search Campaign

**Prepared for:** Einhorn Barbarito
**Prepared by:** Montclair Digital
**Date:** March 2026

---

## Background

During the conquest campaign targeting SKM Law, generic client acquisition search terms were tested alongside the conquest keywords. Performance of these generic keywords was strong and suggests an opportunity to generate new client leads from search beyond the SKM campaign.

---

## Campaign Objective

Generate qualified legal leads through Google search advertising by targeting high-intent generic legal queries relevant to the firm's practice areas.

**Success metrics:**

Search performance:
- Impressions
- Click-through rate
- Cost per click

Lead generation:
- Website contact form submissions
- Phone calls originating from the website

---

## Measurement and Attribution

Uses Google tracking tags implemented on the Einhorn website. Tracks which searches generate site visits and which visits convert via form submissions or phone calls.

**Important:** Lead conversion depends on two factors:
- Quality of the initial visit (search intent alignment)
- Website conversion effectiveness (how well the site drives contact)

Campaign optimization will focus on both keyword targeting and understanding on-site visitor behavior.

---

## Campaign Scope

Montclair Digital will:
- Expand keyword targeting to include generic client acquisition searches
- Manage Google Ads campaign structure and targeting
- Optimize bidding and keyword selection based on performance
- Monitor search performance and lead generation metrics
- Provide ongoing campaign adjustments during the test period

---

## Timeline

Two-month initial test:
- Month 1: Launch and gather data
- Month 2: Review performance together -- determine whether to increase, reduce, or expand to additional keywords
- Mid-month 2: Scheduled performance review
