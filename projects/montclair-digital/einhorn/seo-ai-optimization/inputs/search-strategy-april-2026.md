# Einhorn Search Strategy
**Source:** Montclair Digital Group
**Date:** April 1, 2026
**Type:** Strategy Document
**Status:** Approved

---

## Overview

Einhorn has a significant opportunity to increase inbound client acquisition by capturing a larger share of existing search demand.

Every day, individuals in New Jersey are actively searching for legal representation across family law and related areas. The opportunity is not to create demand, but to ensure that Einhorn appears more prominently and more consistently when those searches occur.

This strategy focuses on increasing the visibility and authority of the Einhorn website so that:

- More potential clients find the firm
- More of that traffic converts into qualified inquiries
- The firm becomes a trusted presence across key legal topics over time

The approach is intentionally simple and execution-focused, centered on improving how often the firm appears in search, how it is presented to potential clients, and how effectively it converts visitors into leads.

---

## Core Strategy

Three primary workstreams:

1. **Core Pages** -- Ensure the right service pages exist and convert effectively
2. **Answer Engine** -- Publish helpful, relevant content consistently
3. **Inbound Links** -- Increase the number of reputable sites referencing Einhorn

Together, these drive visibility, trust, and lead generation.

---

## Focus Areas

Not all practice areas generate the same level of search-driven demand. Priority order:

1. **Family Law** (primary) -- Aligns most strongly with high-intent client searches and near-term lead generation
2. **Estate and Probate** (secondary) -- Natural expansion
3. **Personal Injury** (controlled test) -- High value, competitive landscape; targeted and controlled approach

This is not intended to deprioritize other practice areas, but to concentrate initial efforts where digital channels can drive the most immediate and measurable impact. As momentum builds, the strategy can expand to additional practices in a structured and scalable way.

---

## Workstream 1: Core Pages (On-Site Foundation)

**Goal:** Ensure strong, high-converting pages exist for key services.

**Key Pages:**
- Divorce Lawyer (NJ)
- Child Custody Lawyer
- Alimony / Support

**Tasks:**
- Create or refine core pages
- Align headlines with search intent
- Add trust signals (experience, tone, outcomes)
- Ensure prominent contact options (phone + forms)
- Hone tracking and reporting

**Outcome:** Pages that capture high-intent traffic and convert visitors into leads.

---

## Workstream 2: Answer Engine (Visibility + Authority)

Designed to ensure Einhorn appears not only in traditional search results, but also in AI-generated responses and informational queries.

**Goal:** Develop and publish a library of high-quality answers to real client questions.

**Example content topics:**
- How is alimony calculated in NJ?
- What happens during a divorce?
- How long does custody take?

**Tasks:**
1. Build a structured answer library
2. Publish consistently
3. Create on-site cross links
4. Distribute widely with links back to primary source

**Outcome:** Expanded visibility, increased trust, and improved search performance.

---

## Workstream 3: Inbound Links (Authority + Rankings)

**Goal:** Increase the number of reputable websites linking to Einhorn.

**Categories:**
- Legal directory listings (Avvo, Justia, FindLaw)
- Local business listings
- Media mentions / PR opportunities
- External content contributions

**Outcome:** Improved rankings, credibility, and visibility across search and AI platforms.
