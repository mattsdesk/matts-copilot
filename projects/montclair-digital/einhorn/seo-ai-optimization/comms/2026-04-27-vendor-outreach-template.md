# Vendor Outreach Template -- SEO / AI Search Visibility

**Date:** 2026-04-27
**Channel:** Web contact forms
**Recipients:** All 10 vendors in CheapyT shortlist (LawRank, MileMark Media, Consultwebs, Rankings.io, iLawyer Marketing, BluShark Digital, Hennessey Digital, Inoriseo, Bizmap LLC, NEWMEDIA.COM)
**Personalization:** One template, light variables
**Ask:** Capabilities + AI proof, then narrow to call shortlist

## Message

Hi,

I'm helping a regional New Jersey law firm evaluate potential partners for SEO and AI search visibility.

The firm wants to grow qualified inbound demand, with an initial focus on family law and high-intent local searches. We're specifically trying to understand how potential partners are approaching AI search visibility across Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and similar answer-based search experiences.

Before scheduling introductory calls, we're narrowing a broader vendor list down to a smaller group of serious contenders. Clear answers to the questions below will help us decide whether a deeper conversation makes sense.

Could you send a brief response covering the following?

1. Relevant experience: Do you have experience with mid-sized law firms, family law firms, or multi-practice regional firms? Any examples that are reasonably comparable?

2. Recommended approach: At a high level, how would you approach the first 90 days for a regional NJ law firm focused initially on family law?

3. AI search visibility: How are you currently helping clients improve visibility in AI-generated answers across Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and similar tools?

4. Measurement: How do you measure success beyond rankings and traffic? Specifically, how do you connect SEO or AI visibility work to phone calls, form submissions, consultations, and signed clients?

5. Content and authority building: How do you handle legal content strategy, attorney review, local authority, off-site visibility, and backlink and citation development?

6. Working model: How would you typically work alongside an existing website or PR partner if the firm already has one?

7. Pricing and structure: What is your typical monthly range, setup cost if any, contract length, and recommended budget for a firm like this?

8. Timeline and expectations: What should we realistically expect at 3, 6, and 12 months?

If there's a fit after reviewing the above, we can schedule a focused follow-up call.

Best,
Matt
