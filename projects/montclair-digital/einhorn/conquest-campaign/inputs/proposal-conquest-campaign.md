# Proposal: Einhorn Conquest Search Ads

**Prepared by:** Montclair Digital
**Status:** Approved (assumed -- in active use)

---

## Program Summary

Targeted paid search program designed to intercept individuals actively researching or considering SKM Law (a newly formed competing law firm). The program targets:

- Searches for the competing firm's name and related variations
- Searches indicating interest in switching representation to that firm
- Decision-stage queries where individuals are actively evaluating a switch

Strategy: paid search placement against agreed keyword categories, directing users to authoritative educational content on Einhorn's website. Objective is to introduce consideration and hesitation at the moment a switching decision is being contemplated, reinforcing the value of stability and continuity.

---

## Scope

Paid search promotion of website content (educational article) against:
- Competitor-specific keywords (SKM Law name and variations, subject to platform rules)
- Switching-intent queries
- Decision-stage comparison searches

Ads promote the educational article rather than direct firm marketing.
