The Opportunity

Renna Media is more than a newspaper business. It is a trusted local distribution platform, an advertiser relationship engine, and a community brand built over decades. The asset is the place Renna holds in 30 communities, not the printing press.

Core Thesis

Protect the print trust engine, digitize the operating system, and add higher-margin digital services for local advertisers.

Careful Expansion

Add towns only after unit economics and staffing are proven.

The Three-Year Roadmap
Year One

Stabilize, Learn, Modernize

  • Protect advertiser relationships
  • Document the monthly publication cycle
  • Modernize design and layout tools
  • Add production support
  • Pilot digital add-ons with existing advertisers
Year Two

Build the Growth Engine

  • Add commission-based sales support
  • Create print + digital advertiser packages
  • Rebuild web and social presence
  • Test adjacent town expansion
  • Sell inserts and EDDM as standalone products
Year Three

Scale the Platform

  • Reach 40 to 50+ towns where the unit economics work
  • Build full local marketing services line
  • Hire a GM and operations lead
  • Explore regional media or advertiser-book acquisitions
  • Print as the trust engine, digital as the margin engine

First 90 Days

  • Learn the full publication cycle hands-on
  • Meet key advertisers and contributors
  • Map town-level economics
  • Document repeatable workflows
  • Build staffing and digital pilot plans
