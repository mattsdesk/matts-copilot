## What This Is

Renna Media publishes 30 hyper-local town newspapers in Essex, Morris, Union, and Somerset counties in NJ. Direct-mailed monthly to every home and business in each town (206,000 monthly mailings). Operated by husband-and-wife team Joe and Tina Renna out of a home office. Retiring.

## Goal

Evaluate as a potential acquisition with two intertwined theses:

1. **Standalone print business.** Stable cash-flow asset that pays for itself and the SBA note while a buyer professionalizes operations.
2. **Montclair Digital integration.** Pre-qualified prospect list of ~120 NJ SMB advertisers plus a monthly distribution channel into 206K homes that can be cross-sold into MD's digital services book.

Key questions:

1. Is advertiser retention real or fragile relationship-driven habit?
2. What is the true normalized SDE after restoring eliminated headcount?
3. What does the customer concentration table actually look like (top 15% = 60% of revenue per CIM)?
4. Is the "scalable launch" claim supported by unit economics on the 6 papers launched in 2024?
5. What is the realistic conversion rate of print advertisers into Montclair Digital retainers?
6. What is the right offer price (target 2.0x-2.8x normalized SDE)?
