## Notes and Tuning Suggestions

1. **BizBuySell email retrieval gap:** The Gmail connector returns only subject-line snippets for BizBuySell alert emails, not full listing bodies. This limits our coverage from what is otherwise a primary discovery source. Consider:
   - Supplementing directly at BizBuySell.com (not via email alerts)
   - Testing whether a different email filter or fetch strategy can capture full BizBuySell bodies
   - Adding BizBuySell direct browsing as a weekly manual supplement

2. **Auto repair as a sector:** The semi-absentee auto shop is a data point on whether owner-removed service businesses fit the thesis. Recommend clarifying the "work ON not IN" bar for inherently skill-dependent trades (mechanic, HVAC, plumbing, etc.). The auto shop should not be promoted unless the owner has genuinely hired and trained technicians who ARE the revenue generator, not the owner.

3. **Source quality note:** East Coast Business Brokers and Synergy continue to be high-volume, lower-fit sources. Most listings they send are food/hospitality (excluded), or out-of-radius, or SDE-mismatched. Consider deprioritizing their mass mailings in favor of manual searches on BizBuySell, BizQuest, or LoopNet filtered to thesis criteria.

4. **Passive Hold pipeline:** No new Passive Holds surfaced this run. Self-storage, car wash, equipment rental, and vending queries via WebSearch returned links to marketplaces but no specific on-market listings with SDE and asking price visible. May need direct marketplace browsing (not Google search) to identify passive hold candidates.
