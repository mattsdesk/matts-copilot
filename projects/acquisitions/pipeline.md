## Status Definitions

- **Inbox:** Captured but not yet triaged
- **Triage:** Scorecard in progress
- **Vet:** Passed triage, deep diligence in progress
- **Graduated:** Serious enough to warrant its own top-level project folder
- **Archived:** Killed. Reason captured in `archive/`.
