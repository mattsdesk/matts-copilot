## Key Findings: What Drives Fandom and Purchases

FOMO effect: people buy exclusive items when they see friends have them. Mystery box appeal drives collection desire. Money-can't-buy experiences provide exclusivity value.

Two core components of fandom: emotionally resonant storytelling and sense of belonging. Subscription tactics deepen connection and belonging.

---

## Revenue Impact and Business Model

Subscriptions not primary P&L drivers for leagues; they act as accelerants on core revenue (broadcast rights, sponsorship, tickets). Higher fandom = higher broadcast and sponsorship value. Brand equity transfer works: established stars build exposure for new/lesser-known players (example: Bryson DeChambeau with Joaquin Niemann).

---

## Storytelling and Content Strategy

Live golf struggles with storytelling despite rich available narratives. Successful league approach: overarching storytelling calendar with all channels (social, broadcast, digital, PR) reinforcing same storylines in their format. Each medium adapts the story. Example: Formula 1's week-long Yuki Tsunoda focus in Japan. NFL model: daily game description updates with latest storylines and context.
