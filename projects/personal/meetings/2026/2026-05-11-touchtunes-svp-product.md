## Product Leadership Philosophy

Product manager as quarterback: drives alignment, doesn't dictate solutions. Business objectives and user experience are mutually reinforcing, not competing. Everything needs measurable ROI: tech debt, UX improvements, revenue features.

"Paper cuts" concept: small UX issues compound to impact retention.

Prioritization through collaborative roadmapping with engineering/design input.
