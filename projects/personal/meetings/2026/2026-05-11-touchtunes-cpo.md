## LiveGolf Product Strategy Case Study

- Inherited broken platform built by vendors, non-functional
- Set North Star: Build fandom through emotionally resonant storytelling plus sense of belonging
- Rebuilt entire app in 72 business days with 4 technical sprints

- Strategy: Foundation first, then utility features, tickets integration last
  - Avoided "pointless app" trap by building value before ticketing complexity
