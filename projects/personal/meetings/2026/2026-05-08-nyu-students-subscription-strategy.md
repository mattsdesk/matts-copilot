## Fandom Framework & Strategy

Two core components of fandom:
1. Emotionally resonant storytelling
2. Sense of belonging

Subscription tactics create deeper connection and belonging.

Revenue impact:
- Not primary P&L drivers for leagues
- Act as accelerants on core revenue streams (broadcast rights, sponsorship, tickets)
- Higher fandom = higher broadcast/sponsorship value

Brand equity transfer opportunities: use established stars to build new/lesser-known players. Example: Bryson DeChambeau time mixed with Joaquin Niemann exposure.

## Storytelling & Content Strategy

Live golf struggles with storytelling despite available narratives:
- Missed opportunities: Niemann vs Rahm championship rivalry
- Historical beef: DeChambeau vs Koepka could drive engagement

Successful league approach:
1. Overarching storytelling calendar
2. All channels (social, broadcast, digital, PR) reinforce same storylines
3. Each medium adapts story to their format
- Example: Formula 1's week-long Yuki Tsunoda focus in Japan

NFL model: Daily updates to game descriptions with latest storylines/context.
