## Caroline's Background & Approach

**Prior Role:** Product leader for US sports portfolio at WBD. Based in Seattle/Bellevue area.

**Philosophy:** Inherited "cast-offs and rejects" team at Fubo, transformed them into high performers through:
- Matching individual skills to business needs rather than org structure
- Focusing team around key business levers, not vanity metrics
- Developed reputation as "people whisperer" for growing weaker candidates

**Key Project Example - Marvel Unlimited Rebuild:**
- Ground-up rebuild of 15-year-old comic subscription platform (30K comics)
- User research at Comic-Con due to no budget for formal studies
- Key insight: Two distinct use cases for "My Library" feature (queue vs achievement tracking)
- Critical discoveries across three core screens:
  - Library: Need flexibility for user-created solutions
  - Search: Complex entity structure (titles, numbers, dates, authors)
  - Reader: Panel-by-panel vs full-page reading modes misunderstood
- Results: Historic high subscriber rates, increased library usage, decreased time-to-book-open

**Fubo Retention Focus:**
- Identified 90% of video starts from 3 locations: top carousel, in-video player, TV guide
- Restructured team around key business levers for minutes watched optimization
- Transformed product culture from reactive to strategic
