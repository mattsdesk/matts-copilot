## Data Analysis and Leadership Approach

Matt's philosophy from Fubo TV: "Leading from the back"
- Empowers team with trust and ownership
- Sets North Star metrics, guides execution

Primary metric: Minutes watched (strong correlation with retention)

Recommendation approach:
- Lookalike audience selection most effective
- Balance subscription content vs. FAST channels (higher ad revenue)
- Constant A/B testing on critical carousels (90% of selections in 3 carousels)

Three-pronged approach to decisions:
1. Empowered, engaged teams (not "Excel jockeys")
2. Qualitative research for "why" behind data patterns
3. Collective decades of experience for judgment calls

Statistical significance requires understanding root causes (e.g., personalization impact from names vs. minor wording changes).
