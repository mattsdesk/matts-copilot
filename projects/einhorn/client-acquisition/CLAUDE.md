# Sub-Project: Client Acquisition
**Parent Project:** Einhorn
**Type:** Client -- Paid Search
**Status:** Active
**Owner:** Matt Saunders
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-17

---

## Role
Generate new qualified leads by expanding into generic, non-branded search demand. Focus is on scaling lead volume while maintaining acceptable efficiency (CPL, conversion rate).

This sub-project covers generic and non-branded terms only. Competitor-branded terms belong in Conquest Campaign.

## Goals
- Generate qualified client leads at a scalable CPL
- Expand keyword coverage across non-branded, intent-driven search terms
- Optimize for lead quality, not just volume
- Balance spend efficiency with growth targets

## Current State
- Campaign in early test phase -- two-month initial test authorized at $5,000/month ($10,000 total)
- Originated from strong performance signals on generic keywords tested during the conquest campaign
- Google tracking tags implemented on Einhorn website (form submissions + phone calls being tracked)
- No CPL data yet -- to be populated as performance data becomes available
- Mid-month-two review scheduled to determine: increase, reduce, or expand keyword scope

## Key Context
- Keyword focus: high-intent generic legal queries relevant to Einhorn's practice areas (family law, employment law)
- Targeting approach: generic and non-branded search demand only -- no competitor terms
- Landing destination: Einhorn website (standard site, not a dedicated landing page)
- Conversion tracking: Google tags on contact form submissions and phone calls
- Budget consistent with conquest campaign ($5,000/month) -- provides comparable baseline for evaluation
- Performance depends on both traffic quality and website conversion effectiveness
- Full proposal on file: `/inputs/proposal-client-acquisition.md`


---

## Execution Steps

### 1. Identify Opportunity
Review results from prior generic keyword tests, existing campaign data, and client goals. Identify which keyword categories show promise and where expansion is justified.

### 2. Build Keyword Strategy
Focus on:
- High-intent legal queries
- Family law-related searches
- Local modifiers

Group keywords into tightly themed clusters with clear intent categories.

### 3. Define Campaign Structure
Recommend campaign segmentation, ad group structure, and targeting approach. Ensure structure is clear, scalable, and easy to optimize.

### 4. Define Measurement Approach
Track clicks and CPC, conversion rate, and leads (calls and forms).

Explicitly state: performance depends on both traffic quality and website conversion effectiveness. Not all outcomes are controllable via media alone.

### 5. Balance Scale vs Efficiency
Recommend how to expand volume, where to control costs, and how to prioritize high-value queries.

### 6. Recommend Actions
Provide:
- Keyword expansion plan
- Campaign structure recommendation
- Optimization approach

### 7. Output Format
Outputs should include:
- Opportunity summary
- Keyword strategy
- Campaign structure
- Measurement plan
- Assumptions and dependencies

---

## Execution Rules
- Prioritize qualified leads over traffic volume
- Avoid overly broad keywords without clear intent
- Do not assume conversion performance is fully controllable
- Tie all recommendations to lead generation

---

## Default Mindset
Operate as a growth marketer and demand generator.

The goal is not clicks. The goal is scalable, qualified lead generation.
