**Type:** Client
**Status:** Active

## Brief
Einhorn is a client engagement covering all digital marketing work across three active channels: SEO and AI optimization, conquest search advertising, and client acquisition search advertising. The core objective is generating qualified client leads through search visibility, competitive capture, and scalable acquisition.

## Goals
- Generate qualified client leads for Einhorn
- Build and maintain organic search visibility and AI surface presence
- Capture competitor-branded search demand efficiently
- Scale generic search-driven lead generation

## Overview
This project is structured into three independent sub-projects, each with a distinct role. Work within each sub-project must stay aligned under a single client strategy. Insights from one sub-project should inform others when relevant.

## Operating Instructions

### 1. Anchor to Client Context
Before executing any task, read:
- `client-profile.md` -- who Einhorn is, their business, their targets
- `memory.md` -- strategic decisions, campaign history, prior context

Do not operate in isolation within a sub-project.

### 2. Identify the Relevant Sub-Project
All work must map to one of the three sub-projects. If unclear, ask before proceeding.

### 3. Maintain Separation of Concerns

**SEO and AI Optimization**
- Improve organic visibility and AI/search surface presence
- Focus: content, structure, discoverability
- Does not cover: paid media

**Conquest Campaign**
- Capture demand from competitor-branded searches
- Focus: efficiency, defensive positioning, CPC/impression share/conversion behavior
- Does not cover: generic or non-branded demand

**Client Acquisition**
- Expand into generic, non-branded search demand
- Focus: lead volume, scaling, volume vs efficiency balance
- Does not cover: branded or competitor-branded terms

### 4. Connect Insights Across Sub-Projects
When insights from one sub-project have cross-impact, note it in `memory.md`. Examples:
- Strong keyword performance in conquest -> input into acquisition strategy
- SEO gaps -> inform paid search landing pages
- Conversion issues -> affect all channels

---

## Execution Rules
- Be direct and outcome-focused
- Prioritize actions that drive leads
- Avoid generic recommendations
- Tie all recommendations to observed data or clear logic
- Separate traffic generation from conversion performance
