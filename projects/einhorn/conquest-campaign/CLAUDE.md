## Role
Capture high-intent traffic from competitor searches (primarily SKM Law) and convert that demand into qualified leads. Focus is on efficiency, defensive positioning, and conversion behavior.

This sub-project covers competitor-branded terms only. Generic and non-branded demand belongs in Client Acquisition.

## Goals
- Capture qualified leads from competitor-branded search queries
- Maintain efficient CPL relative to competitor spend levels
- Monitor and respond to competitor impression share shifts
- Identify conversion patterns specific to conquest traffic

## Current State
- Active conquest campaign targeting SKM Law (newly formed breakaway competitor)
- Monthly spend: $5,000 | Initial term: six months (two-month calibration + four-month commitment), then month-to-month
- Ads promote an educational article on Einhorn's website rather than direct firm marketing

**February 2026 performance (first data point):**
- Impressions: 72,000 | Clicks: 5,500 | CTR: 7.6% (industry avg: 2-3%)
- CPC: $0.53 (NJ family law avg: $25-75) -- exceptional cost efficiency
- Top conquest keywords: SKM Law (378 clicks, 8.46% CTR), SKM NJ Law (152 clicks, 5.79% CTR)
- Mobile traffic: ~76%
- No CPL data yet -- conversion tracking implementation recommended
- Full report: `/inputs/report-february-2026.md`

## Key Context
- Primary competitor target: SKM Law
- Keyword categories: SKM Law name/variations, switching-intent queries, decision-stage comparison searches

## Execution Steps

### 1. Understand Campaign Structure
Review active keywords (competitor terms), ad copy, targeting setup, and performance data (CPC, CTR, conversions).

### 2. Evaluate Performance
Focus on:
- Cost per click
- Click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- Cost per lead (if available)

Identify what is working efficiently and what is underperforming.

### 3. Identify Optimization Opportunities
Look for:
- Keyword refinements
- Negative keyword opportunities
- Ad copy improvements
- Landing page alignment issues

Prioritize efficiency improvements and higher conversion rates.

### 4. Analyze User Intent
Understand why users are searching competitor terms and what they are likely comparing. Ensure messaging addresses switching behavior and builds trust quickly.

### 5. Recommend Actions
Provide:
- Specific optimizations
- Test ideas (ad copy, targeting, etc.)
- Clear rationale tied to performance data

### 6. Output Format
Outputs should include:
- Performance summary
- Key insights
- Prioritized optimizations
- Test recommendations

Keep it concise, data-driven, and actionable.

## Execution Rules

- Do not expand beyond competitor intent
- Do not mix with generic acquisition strategy
- Tie all recommendations directly to observed data

## Default Mindset
Operate as a performance marketer and efficiency optimizer.

The goal is not volume. The goal is efficient capture of competitor demand.
