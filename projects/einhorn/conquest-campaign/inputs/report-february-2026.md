# Campaign Report: February 2026
**Campaign:** Google Ads (Conquest + Generic)
**Prepared by:** Montclair Digital
**Period:** February 2026

---

## Top-Line Performance

| Metric | Result | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Total Impressions | 72,000 | -- |
| Total Clicks | 5,500 | -- |
| Click-Through Rate | 7.6% | Industry avg: 2-3% |
| Cost Per Click | $0.53 | NJ family law avg: $25-$75 |

---

## Competitor Keywords (Conquest)

| Keyword | Impressions | Clicks | CTR |
|---|---|---|---|
| SKM Law | 4,470 | 378 | 8.46% |
| SKM NJ Law | 2,623 | 152 | 5.79% |
| Snyder Kenney & Mercado | 765 | 59 | 7.71% |

**Insight:** Competitor terms successfully generated targeted, high-intent traffic. Volume is naturally limited -- this is a defined audience researching a specific firm.

---

## Generic Keywords (Acquisition)

| Keyword | Impressions | Clicks | CTR |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lawyers office near me | 21,413 | 2,133 | 9.96% |
| Family law lawyers near me | 18,517 | 771 | 4.16% |
| Family estate lawyers near me | 3,374 | 642 | 19.03% |
| Property lawyers near me | 8,635 | 540 | 6.25% |

**Standout:** "Family estate lawyers near me" -- 19.03% CTR, nearly 10x industry average. High-priority segment for expansion.

---

## Device Behavior

- Mobile: ~76%
- Desktop: ~24%

Consistent with national trends for local legal searches. Underscores importance of mobile-optimized landing pages and contact forms.

---

## Key Insights

1. **Competitor targeting worked.** Ads appeared at the right moment in the decision process.
2. **Cost efficiency was exceptional.** $0.53 CPC vs $25-75 NJ family law average.
3. **Generic searches are the growth lever.** "Lawyers near me" and related terms generated highest click volume and are scalable.
4. **Mobile-first behavior confirmed.** 76% mobile -- fast, easy contact experience will directly improve conversion.

---

## Recommendations

**Conquest (Defensive)**
- Continue targeting SKM competitor keywords
- Increase bid caps slightly to protect top placement

**Client Acquisition (Growth)**
- Expand targeting of high-intent searches ("lawyers near me", estate planning terms)
- Implement conversion tracking to measure actual leads from ad traffic
- Build mobile-optimized contact form to reduce friction
- Develop follow-up campaigns (email, retargeting) to nurture non-converting visitors
