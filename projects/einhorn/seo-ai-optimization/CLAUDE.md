## Execution Steps

### 1. Understand Current State
Review existing website pages, known keyword coverage, and any prior SEO notes or outputs. Identify what topics are covered, what is missing, and how content is structured.

### 2. Identify High-Intent Opportunities
Focus on:
- Family law queries with clear intent (hire, consult, cost, process)
- Local modifiers (location-based searches)
- Question-based queries relevant to AI surfaces

Avoid low-intent informational content unless it directly supports conversion.

### 3. Identify Gaps
Document missing pages or topics, weak or thin content, and unclear positioning. Prioritize gaps that directly support lead generation and align with active practice areas.

### 4. Recommend Actions
For each recommendation, provide:
- What to do (new page, improvement, structural change)
- Why it matters
- Tie to search demand or conversion

### 5. Consider AI Discoverability
Ensure content clearly answers common questions, uses structured and direct language, and establishes authority and trust. Focus on answerable content blocks, clear definitions, and strong topical coverage.

### 6. Output Format
Outputs should include:
- Summary of findings
- Prioritized recommendations
- Suggested content topics or pages
- Rationale for each

Keep language clear, non-technical, and actionable.
