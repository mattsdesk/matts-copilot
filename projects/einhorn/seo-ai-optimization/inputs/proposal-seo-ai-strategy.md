# Proposal: SEO & AI Discovery Strategy Leadership

**Prepared by:** Montclair Digital
**Engagement Type:** Ongoing advisory
**Status:** Proposed

---

## Overview and Objective

Client discovery is evolving. Prospective clients find and compare law firms through traditional search engines and AI-driven tools. Visibility is driven by clarity, authority, and consistency across the firm's digital presence.

**Objective:** Provide senior-level SEO and AI discovery strategy leadership -- ensuring all digital efforts are aligned, prioritized, and compounding toward the firm's business goals. Starting with Personal Injury; extensible to other practices over time.

---

## Role and Approach

**Matt Saunders (via Montclair Digital)** acts as the firm's Digital Search & AI Strategy Lead.

- Advisory in nature -- focused on strategic direction, prioritization, and judgment
- Not day-to-day execution
- Supports and enables internal teams or external agencies (SEO, PR, content, website)
- Single point of digital strategy ownership

---

## Scope of Services (Included)

- SEO & AI discovery strategy and prioritization
- Guidance and review of agency plans and outputs
- Off-site authority and content direction
- On-site strategy and website guidance (as needed)
- Vendor evaluation, RFP support, and agency transitions if required

## Out of Scope (By Design)

- Content production
- PR pitching or placement execution
- Technical SEO implementation
- Web design or development
- Link building
- Paid media management

---

## Phased Approach

**Phase 1: Direction & Off-Site Momentum**
Align goals, define authority priorities, guide off-site execution to establish early traction and learning.

**Phase 2: On-Site Optimization (If Warranted)**
Apply insights from off-site work to on-site structure, messaging, and potential rebuild considerations.

---

## Governance and Collaboration

Montclair Digital acts as strategic digital lead. Execution agencies retain ownership of delivery. Collaboration via regular reviews, guidance, and prioritization discussions.
