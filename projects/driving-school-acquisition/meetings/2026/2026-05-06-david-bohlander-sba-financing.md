- No lien on home equity

### HELOC vs SBA Comparison

HELOC advantages:
- Better interest rates currently
- Faster closing (30 days vs 45-60 days)

SBA advantages:
- Tax deductible interest
- Fixed amortization schedule
- No prepayment penalty for future refinancing
- Debt stays in business entity vs personal
